Homework 1 CS 360: Data Visualization

Ned Azar

These are the data visualizations that I did in Tableau then implemented into D3.js

Data

My data was provided by Data SF

San Francisco International Airport data on Landings Statistics (DATASF).
I used a three column data set

  1. Month This column gives me the month that the flight occured
  2. Domestic This column give me the sum of all the passenger counts on Domestic flights
  3. International This column give me the sum of all the passenger counts on International flights

Wrangling

I really only cared about the passenger count, the month the flight occured, and whether it was domestic or international.
